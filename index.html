The Festival

Every year the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival takes place. We're happy to tell you that this year it will be at Brooklyn Bridge Park which holds up to 5,000 people! Everybody is welcome - from kids to retirees, african, chineese or american, let's get together and watch some good movies selected by our film critics.
